About Me

Get to know Kade

Hey, I'm Kade. I'm from Grace, Idaho, which is basically a dot on the map in the southeast corner of the state. My family grows potatoes. I grew up doing all of it: moving pipe, driving tractors, fixing stuff that broke at the worst possible time. Honestly some of my favorite memories are just being out on the farm early in the morning before anyone else is up.

Right now I'm at BYU studying business. I'm a big Cougar sports guy. Football Saturdays at LaVell Edwards are probably my favorite thing about being out here, but I'm at the Marriott Center for basketball games whenever I can be too. Other than that I've gotten really into flying drones lately. This past summer I actually got my Part 107 commercial drone license, so that's been a cool thing to have.

After high school I lived in Colombia for two years doing volunteer work in Barranquilla. That was a big deal for me. I learned Spanish, got to know people from all sorts of different backgrounds, and honestly just grew up a lot. I still think about the food there all the time. Arepas are no joke.

Outside of school and sports, I'm usually playing basketball, hiking, skiing, or camping. In the summers I go back home and work on the farm. I also like messing around in Excel more than most people probably should, and I've been getting more and more into finance and investing stuff on the side.

Anyway, thanks for checking out my site. Feel free to connect with me through the social links below or hit up the Contact page if you want to reach out.
