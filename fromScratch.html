Drones with Kade

FAA Part 107 Certified Commercial Drone Pilot

I got into flying drones a couple years ago and it quickly became one of my favorite hobbies. This past summer I went ahead and got my FAA Part 107 commercial drone license, which means I can legally fly drones for paid work like real estate photos, farm surveying, events, etc. Growing up on a potato farm in Idaho, I've always been around big open spaces, and drones are a pretty cool way to see it all from above.

Why I Like Drones

  • You get to see the world from a completely different angle
  • Great for scouting farmland and checking on crops
  • Useful for real estate, events, and creative projects
  • It's just fun to fly stuff around

My Gear & Goals

  2. Skills I'm Working On
